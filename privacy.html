Ooiy App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2025

Ooiy operates as a global marketplace platform where users can buy and sell products, land, cars, hotel services, food services, and find nearby services like mechanics and plumbers. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

We may collect the following information to operate and improve our services:

  • Name and Email Address
  • Phone Number (Optional: If provided, it will be shown publicly on listings to allow buyer-seller contact)
  • Location Information
  • Device and Usage Data
  • Uploaded Content (such as product listings and service details)

Note: Phone number is not required but, if provided, it becomes publicly visible to help buyers contact sellers directly.

2. Use of Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To display relevant ads and offers
  • To communicate app updates or support
  • To show public contact details in listings where users have provided them voluntarily

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including Google AdMob, Google Firebase, and Google Translate API. These may collect data under their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their terms separately.

4. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. User Rights

You can:

  • Request data deletion
  • Opt out of certain data uses
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Updates will be posted here along with the effective date.

Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Ooiy account and all associated data, you can submit a request by emailing us.

Please include the following in your email:

  • Your registered phone number or email ID
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Once we receive your request, we will process it within 7 days and confirm by email.
